Ugly Olive Rolls

Ingredients

  • All Purpose Flour, 4 cups
  • Semolina Flour, 2 cups
  • Instant Yeast, 3 tsp
  • Kosher Salt, 1 tsp
  • Sugar, 2 tbsp
  • Olive Oil, 4, tbsp
  • Water, 1.5 cups
  • Large Jar of Kalamata Olives
  • Juice from Olives
  • Greasing Agent of Choice (I usually go with shortning)
  • Egg wash

Directions

  1. Mix flours, yeast, salt, sugar, olive oil, water, and juice from yout jar of olives in a large bowl until shaggy. Let it sit in the bowl for half an hour covered with a clean cloth.

  2. While the dough is resting, chop olives in half.

  3. Kneed in the olives, and work the dough until it is smooth and elastic. Rest again for two hours or until the dough has doubled in size.

  4. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease cookie sheet and form rolls. Brush rolls with egg wash.

  5. Bake for 24 minutes or until golden brown.
